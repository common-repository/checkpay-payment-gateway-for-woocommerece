Accept WeChat Pay and Alipay via CheckPay payment gateway in your store. Customers pay in Chinese RMB and you receive Canadian dollars ($CAD).

== Description ==

Checkpay’s payment gateway enables Canadian local merchants to accept Alipay and WeChat Pay on their eCommerce platforms, collecting Canadian dollars ($) from users who pay in Chinese RMB (￥).

= Why choose Checkpay? =
Checkpay Technologies Ltd. (Checkpay) is an authorized payment service provider based in North America, serving international cross-border businesses across different industries. It supports a full range of WeChat Pay and Alipay Online and Offline solutions and services.

Checkpay is a professional payment processor certified by Alipay and WeChat. Checkpay ensures you to have a safe and secure payment solution and aims to provide you seamless experience. We constantly work on improving our cooperation with international acquirers, allowing you to access more alternative payment methods and increase sales revenue.

Checkpay values each customer and is eager to address each of your concerns immediately. Please do not hesitate to [contact us](https://www.checkpay.ca/contacts)if we could be any of your help regarding registration, integration, account setup, technical questions and more.

More information is available on [Checkpay Website](https://www.checkpay.ca).

== Features and Benefits ==
* One plugin integrates WeChat Pay and Alipay. Merchants can enable WeChat Pay and/or Alipay.
* Supports pay through scanning QR Code.
* Accepts full and partial refunds.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where should I check the payment order status? =
1. Login the [Checkpay Merchant Dashboard](https://dashboard.checkpay.ca) with your Login ID and password.
2. On WooCommerce Order Details, find the checkpay_order_id.
3. On Checkpay Merchant Dashboard, search the Platform Order Id you found on WooCommerce Order Details and check the order status.
